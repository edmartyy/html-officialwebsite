GingerBread Latte-Tini

INGREDIENTS


  1. 35ml Bacardi Oakheart rum
  2. 15ml Kahlua coffee liqueur
  3. 15ml gingerbread syrup
  4. 50ml espresso
  5. 25ml cream
  6. Dusting of chocolate powder

Method

  1. Add ingredients to a cocktail mixer and shake with ice to mix
  2. Pour through a strainer into a coupe glass
  3. Garnish with a dusting of chocolate powder and serve
