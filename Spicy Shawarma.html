Spicy Shawarma Chicken Wrap Recipe

INGREDIENTS


  1. 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  2. 2-4 tortilla wraps or paratha breads
  3. Olive oil, for frying
  4. 2 spring onions, trimmed and finely shredded
  5. ¼ head of pointed cabbage, finely shredded

FOR THE MARINADE

  1. ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  2. ½ tsp ground ginger
  3. ½ tsp ground coriander
  4. Seeds from 3 cardamom pods, ground in a mortar
  5. Pinch of freshly grated nutmeg
  6. 2 garlic cloves, peeled
  7. Juice of ¼ lemon
  8. Small bunch of coriander, leaves chopped
  9. Olive oil
  10. Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Toy Serve

  1. Mayonnaise
  2. Chilli sauce or sauce of your choice

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Make the marinade by blending spices, garlic, lemon juice, and coriander. Add 5 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper, and mix well. Coat chicken in the marinade and let it sit for up to 2 hours.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200°C/Gas 6. Heat a griddle pan over medium heat. Cook the chicken for 3-4 minutes per side until charred but not burnt. Transfer to a roasting tray and finish cooking in the oven for 5-8 minutes. Let the chicken rest.
  3. Grill the tortilla wraps in batches on the griddle for 2 minutes per side, keeping them warm.
  4. Slice the chicken into bite-sized pieces and place on each warm wrap. Top with spring onions, shredded cabbage, mayonnaise, and chili sauce. Roll up and serve.
