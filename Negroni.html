Truffle Negroni

INGREDIENTS


  1. Thin slice of truffle
  2. 25ml Martini Rosso
  3. 25ml Campari
  4. 25ml Maker's Mark bourbon
  5. 1 1/2 tsp truffle oil

Method

  1. Add all ingredients into the mixing glass
  2. Stir gently for 10 seconds
  3. Single strain over fresh ice
  4. Garnish with a thin slice of truffle
