Love Potion Cocktail

INGREDIENTS


  1. 35ml Croft Pink port
  2. 20ml lemon juice
  3. 10ml sugar syrup
  4. 3 fresh raspberries
  5. 1 fresh mint sprig
  6. Ice cubes, for shaker
  7. Champagne or prosecco, to top up

Method

  1. Put the port, lemon juice, sugar syrup and two of the raspberries in a cocktail shaker and shake well.
  2. Strain the mixture into a tall glass and top up with Champagne or prosecco.
  3. Pour from a low height into a frozen martini glass
  4. Garnish with the remaining raspberry and fresh mint and serve.
