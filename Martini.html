Christmas Martini

INGREDIENTS


  1. 50ml Christmas vodka
  2. 12.5ml Amaretto
  3. 1 tsp Gingerbread syrup
  4. 2 dashes of orange bitters

Christmas Vodka (makes 1 bottle)

  1. Zest of half an orange
  2. 4 cloves
  3. 1/3 cinnamon stick
  4. 1 slice of fresh ginger
  5. Half a grated nutmeg
  6. 15ml maple syrup

Garnish:

  1. Flames orange zest
  2. Candy cane

Glass

  1. Martini glass

Method

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass
  2. Stir with ice
  3. Pour from a low height into a frozen martini glass
  4. Flame an orange zest over the drink then discard
  5. Hang candy cane on the rim of the glass
