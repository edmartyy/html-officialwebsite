HOLIDAY LEMON-HERB CHICKEN THIGHS WITH A CRISPY BACON GRAVY

INGREDIENTS


  1. Lemon-Herb Chicken Thighs
  2. 8 each bone-in chicken thighs (about 3-4 pounds)
  3. Flaky sea salt and fresh cracked pepper, to taste
  4. Olive oil, as needed
  5. 2-3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  6. Pinch of chili flakes, to taste
  7. 1 lemon, zested

Bacon-Apple Cider Gravy

  1. 4 slices, thick cut bacon, sliced into ½ inch pieces
  2. ½ cup diced yellow onion
  3. 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  4. 1-12 ounce bottle of dry hard cider
  5. 2 cups warm chicken stock, divided
  6. 2 lemons, divided
  7. 3-4 sprigs of fresh thyme
  8. 2 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  9. Flaky sea salt and fresh cracked pepper, to taste

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 425˚F.
  2. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat and sear thighs skin-side down until caramelized (3-5 minutes). Dust with flour, then season with salt, pepper, chili flakes, and lemon zest. Flip and cook for 2-3 minutes, then set aside.
  3. For the gravy, cook bacon in the same pan until crisp, then add onions and garlic, cooking until fragrant (1 minute). Add lemon wedges and fresh herbs, cooking for 1 more minute. Deglaze with cider, then stir in hot chicken stock and simmer until reduced by half.
  4. Return chicken to the pan, skin-side up, nestling in the gravy. Zest the remaining lemon over the chicken, then season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes. Roast in the oven for 20 minutes or until crispy and juices run clear. Serve with lemon slices and fresh herbs.
