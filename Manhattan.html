Truffle Manhattan

INGREDIENTS


  1. 20ml Martini Rosso
  2. 50ml Macallan Gold
  3. 1 1/2 tsps truffle oil
  4. 3 drops Angostura bitters
  5. Orange peel

Method

  1. Add all wet ingredients in a mixing glass
  2. Stir gently for 30 seconds
  3. Double strain into a Martini glass
  4. Garnish with a orange peel
