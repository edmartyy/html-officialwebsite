SHEET PAN FOCACCIA PIZZA WITH CRUSHED SAN MARZANOS & MOZZARELLA

INGREDIENTS


  1. 1 cup (250g) bread flour
  2. 1 cup (250g) all-purpose flour
  3. 1 tablespoon coarse semolina (optional)
  4. 3½ teaspoons kosher salt
  5. 1 tablespoon (14g or 2 packages) active dry yeast
  6. 1⅓ cup (320ml) lukewarm water*
  7. 3 tablespoons (50ml) extra-virgin olive oil
  8. 2 teaspoons honey
  9. Flaky maldon sea salt
  10. Fresh chopped rosemary or thyme, optional
  11. Roasted Garlic Butter, optional (recipe in Notes)

Crushed San Marzano Sauce:

  1. 28-ounce can of whole San Marzano tomatoes, drained
  2. 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  3. 4 garlic cloves, minced or grated fine
  4. ½ cup fresh basil leaves (½-ounce clamshell)
  5. 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  6. ¾ teaspoon kosher sea salt
  7. Freshly cracked black pepper

For the Pizza:

  1. 1 cup grated mozzarella (low-moisture)
  2. 1 cup grated Asiago or Romano cheese
  3. ⅓ cup Parmigiano Reggiano
  4. 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  5. 4 ounces fresh mozzarella ciliegine or burrata pearls, torn in half
